## Visual rules (non-negotiable)

### Typography (logo + headers)
- **Chunky 3D BLOCK letters** — bold, square-ish, dimensional. No thin
  sans-serifs. No condensed fonts. No script.
- **Strong 3D extrusion** — letters look like they were dropped into place.
  The extruded depth is its own gradient (warm orange-to-red for "DROP",
  warm gold-to-red for "4"). The extrusion is part of the brand mark.
- **Thick dark navy outline** (~3-5px) hugging the silhouette tightly.
- **Letter face gradient:** white at top → cool sky blue mid → deeper steel
  blue at the bottom of the face. (The "4" character uses a warm gold-to-
  orange-to-red face gradient instead — it's the brand accent.)
- **White rim highlight** along the upper edge of each letter.
- **Sparkle stars** — small, subtle, around the lower edge only. Never
  overlap the letters.

### Buttons
- **Glossy 3D pill** shape — chunky rounded rectangle, rounded ends.
- **Strong color gradient** for the button face (orange / purple / teal /
  green per variant), with a top specular highlight and bottom rim shadow
  giving the pill 3D depth.
- **No painted scenes inside the button face.** No riveted plates, no
  hammered metal. Just a clean glossy gradient pill.
- **Bold white label** with strong contrast against the gradient.
- **Optional small icon** on the left (chevron, trophy silhouette, group
  silhouette) — same chunky 3D style as the logo.
- **Same dark navy outline** (~3px) hugging the pill silhouette.

### Backgrounds
- **Calm cinematic atmosphere** stays — bg-home, bg-shop, bg-career, etc.
  These don't compete with the chunky logo + buttons. They recede.
- Cool palette stays (deep navy with one warm amber accent). Don't change.

### Color rules
- **Primary brand**: white-cyan blue (logo letters), warm gold-orange-red
  (the "4" + warm depth shadow), dark navy (outlines + bg base).
- **Mode buttons**: orange (PLAY), purple (CAREER), teal (LOCAL PLAY).
  Saturated, not muted.
- **Currency**: gold (coins), cyan/blue (gems).
- **Premium accents**: gold #FFD700 only.
- **NEVER**: chrome silver, monochrome, neon-only, pastel.

### Composition
- **Center-weighted vignette** stays for backgrounds.
- **Logo dominates** the upper portion of the home screen — don't shrink it
  to a small icon.
- **Sparkle stars** appear around the logo and behind hero CTAs only,
  sparingly.

## Hard negatives — never include

- NO chrome / metallic-silver / industrial-steel finishes
- NO thin sans-serif typography (no Helvetica-style logos)
- NO sleek minimalist treatments
- NO Connect 4 disc REPLACING any letter (we tried this, it felt clever
  but didn't read as the concept Devon approved)
- NO riveted metal plates as button surfaces
- NO hammered-metal textures as button surfaces
- NO halos, no aura glow behind the logo
- NO drop shadow on the logo (the 3D extrusion IS the depth — additional
  drop shadow stacks)
- NO childish balloon font (chunky ≠ balloon)
